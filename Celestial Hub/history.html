History

Space history refers to the chronological record of human activities and endeavors related to
the exploration and understanding of outer space. It encompasses a wide range of events, achievements, and milestones in the field of astronomy, astrophysics, and space exploration. Space history begins with early observations of celestial bodies and the development of scientific theories about the cosmos.
