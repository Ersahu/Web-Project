Reusable Rocket Technology

Reusable rockets, also known as reusable launch vehicles, are rockets that recover and reuse expensive rocket boosters and other parts. Reusable rockets can be up to 65% cheaper to refurbish and relaunch than building a new rocket

CubeSats and Small Satellites

CubeSats are a subclass of small satellites, which are a type of spacecraft. CubeSats are miniature satellites that are about the size of a Rubik's cube and weigh about 1 kg. They are used for a variety of purposes, including remote sensing and communications.



Space Robots

Space robots are specialized robots that help and replace humans in space exploration, scientific experiments, and other activities. They can also support astronauts by repairing machinery and providing AI-powered assistance.

Space Tourism

Space tourism is a commercial activity that involves traveling into space for recreational purposes. It can also include watching a rocket launch, stargazing, or traveling to a space-centric destination.
